Reverse Ouija

A fun and spooky game to test your reading comprehension capabilty

Instructions

  1. Read the paragraph being presented by the ghost.
  2. It is narrating his story to you. READ CAREFULL!!
  3. 10 questions will follow up next.
  4. Carefully answer each of them.
  5. In case of failure, be ready to face the ghost itself.
  6. Try to kill it in given time or your game will be over.
  7. Clear all 10 question in order to win and get away from spook easily!!
